When you first go on this website, the site road map can guide you quickly through the structure of the website and the author’s habits of classification…

The basic structure of this website is as above picture:

Functionality:

  • Language switcher - used to switch between English and Chinese blogs.

  • Classification Switcher - from different perspective to classify all the articles in site, such as the Series, Categories, Tags, Archives…

  • Search in Site - get related blogs by searching keywords within this website.

  • Social Links - get in touch with the author by other various social links.

  • Share and Favorites - bookmarks your favorite articles by Ctrl+D to local browser, or share articles to different social media.

  • Article Comments - makes you directly communicate with the author within this website.

Series:

The series means to publish collections of articles that related to specific tool within testing technical stack which will be starting from introduction and end to comprehensive practice.

  • User Manual - delivers necessary information about the author and the website.

  • Migrant Stories - will post or forward migrant stories in New Zealand. (in planning …)

  • Religion and Philosophy - will post or forward excellent articles related to religion or philosophy. (in planning …)

  • Postman API Automation Practice - is a series of Postman API automation practices. (coming soon …)

  • Cypress UI Automation Practice - is a series of Cypress UI automation practices. (in planning …)

  • Appium Mobile Automation Practice - is a series of Appium mobile automation practices. (in planning …)

  • JMeter Performance Testing Practice - is a series of JMeter performance testing practices. (in planning …)

Categories:

Not like the series, categories will collect more unsystematic knowledge points, and part of these articles can be components of a specific series in the future.

  • Functional Testing - Original or forwarded articles related to functional testing.

  • API Automation - Original or forwarded articles related to API automation testing.

  • UI Automation - Original or forwarded articles related to UI automation testing.

  • Mobile Automation - Original or forwarded articles related to mobile automation testing.

  • Performance Testing - Original or forwarded articles related to performance testing.

  • DevOps - Original or forwarded articles related to DevOps.

Tags:

The classification of ‘Tags’ often down to a specific tool. The articles under ‘Tags’ has a clear knowledge point, and can be reproduced by anyone.

  • Postman - Original or forwarded articles related to Postman.

  • Cypress- Original or forwarded articles related to Cypress.

  • Cucumber- Original or forwarded articles related to Cucumber.

  • Selenium- Original or forwarded articles related to Selenium.

  • Appium - Original or forwarded articles related to Appium.

  • JMeter- Original or forwarded articles related to JMeter.

  • Jenkins- Original or forwarded articles related to Jenkins.

Archives:

The classification of ‘Archives’ will archive all the articles in the website, and display them by publishing time.
